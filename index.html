You can’t make more hours in each day. Yet you can put more purpose and focus and meaning in each hour. Blaming a lack of time is like blaming the sun for rising. There’s nothing you can do about it, so it’s not a factor to concern yourself with. What you can do, is make the commitment to yourself to make each moment count for something meaningful and valuable. What you can do, is allow your authentic purpose to guide your focus, your actions and your use of the time that’s at hand.

每天都是24小时，然而你可以在每个小时内获得更多的目的，重点和意义。责备时间不足就像责备太阳升起一样。没有什么可以做的，所以这不是一个关心自己的因素。你所能做的，就是对自己作出承诺，使每一个时刻都算在有意义和有价值的事情上。你可以做什么，是让你真实的目的，引导你的重点，你的行动，你的使用时间。

You can complain, fret and worry about all the problems in your life, but you’ll be wasting your time. Or, you can choose to focus on why and how life is so good, and on what you can do to take that goodness and make it even better. Not only is life good, it’s uniquely good for you in your very own way. The possibilities for expressing your purpose are limited only by your imagination. Remind yourself often of the great and wonderful value that you already, always have. Life is good, and in this moment that’s bursting with possibilities, life is yours.

你可以抱怨，烦恼和担心你生活中的所有问题，但是你会浪费你的时间。或者，你可以选择把重点放在生活如此美好的原因和方式上，以及如何做才能把善良变得更好。生活不仅好，而且对你自己来说也是独一无二的。表达你的目的的可能性仅限于你的想象力。时刻提醒自己已经拥有的伟大和美好的价值。生活是美好的，在这个充满可能性的时刻，生活就是你的。
